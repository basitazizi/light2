Services

Full Lighting Services

Everything that used to be compressed into the homepage now lives here: installs, repair work, bistro lights, pathway layouts, and night-demo planning.

New 12V Installation

Fresh low-voltage landscape lighting design and installation tailored to architecture, pathways, planting, and nighttime visibility.

  • Includes: transformer planning, wiring layout, fixture selection
  • Best for: full-property lighting upgrades and new outdoor builds

Existing System Repair

Diagnosis and cleanup for dim fixtures, failed connections, damaged cable runs, overloaded transformers, and inconsistent zones.

  • Includes: troubleshooting, fixture replacement, aiming corrections
  • Best for: aging or partially working systems

Bistro & String Lights

Patio and entertaining-space string lighting with clean support planning and warm ambient coverage.

  • Includes: span layout, anchor planning, visual balance
  • Best for: patios, pergolas, and outdoor dining zones

Tree & Garden Uplighting

Focused accent lighting for trunks, palms, planting beds, and facade-adjacent landscape elements.

  • Includes: beam spread planning and nighttime focal-point layout
  • Best for: curb appeal and layered yard depth

Pathway & Step Lighting

Safer transitions across walkways, garden paths, stairs, and entry routes without blowing out the whole yard.

  • Includes: spacing, glare control, visibility planning
  • Best for: access routes and front-entry guidance

Consultation & Demo Planning

On-site review before buildout so the lighting plan fits the property, goals, and budget.

  • Includes: walkthrough, service recommendation, next-step planning
  • Best for: owners deciding between repair, upgrade, or new install
